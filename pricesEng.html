Pricing

The price is based on the particularities of each shoot: type, duration, necessary gear and post-production. If the shoot takes place outside Sofia, transportation and stay expenses are added.

The general prices are as follows:

Event/photo session Starting from 80 levs for the first hour and 50 levs per every next hour
Wedding(8h) Strating from 360lv.
Individual photo session during an event 100 levs/hour

The price includes editing of each image and the reception of a widest selection of images in digital form. Depending on the nature of the shoot, this price is negotiable.Please, contact me to receive a personal offer.
